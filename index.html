Controls

Move your character with "wasd" or the arrow keys

Aim with your mouse and shoot by pressing left-click

Press "r" to reload your gun

Gameplay

Monsters spawn in waves and you have to shoot them to defeat them

Monsters sometimes drop health when they die which the player can pick up to increase their own health-meter

Green ammo boxes randomly spawn across the map which the player can pick up to replenish their ammo

If a player runs out of ammo a reloading sequence will begin which lasts around 2 seconds
